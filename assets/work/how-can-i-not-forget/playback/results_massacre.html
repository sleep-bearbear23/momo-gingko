Chinese massacre of 1871 — Wikipedia
The Chinese massacre of 1871 was a racially motivated mass murder on October 24, 1871 in Los Angeles, when a mob of around 500 residents attacked, robbed, and murdered at least 17–20 Chinese residents. It remains one of the largest mass lynchings in American history and a defining event in LA's early civic history.
